Hyderabadi biryani is a popular South Asian rice dish from Hyderabad, India, made with basmati rice and meat, typically goat or chicken.

Ingredients

  • Bone-in Chicken or Mutton seasoned with spices (ginger, garlic, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, onions) & yogurt
  • Basmati Rice
  • Saffron
  • Ghee
  • Curry Leaves
  • Onions
